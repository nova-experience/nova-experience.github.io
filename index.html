What is it

More than an event. An inflection point.

The Nova Experience is Nova's flagship annual gathering — a 3-day immersive experience where City Leaders, Vertical Leaders, and selected members come together in a European destination to connect at a deeper level, challenge each other's thinking, and walk away transformed.

Day 1 — Starting from yourself

Personal introspection and self-awareness. Strangers become allies in hours through vulnerability and genuine openness. The kind of connections LinkedIn can't manufacture.

Day 2 — Focus on society

Panels, debates, and workshops around a world-pressing topic. Past editions tackled AI and the future of work with C-level speakers and industry leaders.

Day 3 — Elevating each other

Members uplift each other's projects, startups, and ideas. You leave with actionable insights, new collaborators, and the momentum to pursue your next big goal.
